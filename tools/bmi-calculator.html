What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measurement that uses your height and weight to assess if your body weight is healthy. It helps determine whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

BMI Formula

BMI = Weight ÷ Height²

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: Can BMI be 100% accurate?
    A: BMI is a general indicator and does not account for muscle mass or bone density.
  • Q: How often should I check BMI?
    A: Regular monitoring every few weeks or months is recommended for tracking progress.
  • Q: Can children use this BMI calculator?
    A: This calculator is designed for adults; children require age-specific BMI charts.
