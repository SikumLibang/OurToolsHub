What is an Age Calculator?

An Age Calculator is an online tool that determines the exact age of a person from their date of birth to a selected date. It calculates age in years, months, and days instantly.

How Does It Work?

The tool calculates the time difference between two dates using accurate calendar logic. It accounts for leap years, different month lengths, and date boundaries to ensure precise results.
