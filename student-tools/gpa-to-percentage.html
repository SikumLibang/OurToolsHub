How to Convert GPA to Percentage?

Converting GPA to percentage depends on your grading scale. The general formula used is:

Percentage = (GPA ÷ Maximum GPA Scale) × 100

Examples

  • 3.5 GPA on 4.0 scale = 87.5%
  • 8.2 GPA on 10.0 scale = 82%
  • 4.5 GPA on 5.0 scale = 90%

Always confirm with your institution, as some universities may apply custom conversion formulas.
