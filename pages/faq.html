Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to the most common questions about OurToolsHub and how our tools work.

Are all tools on OurToolsHub free?

Yes. Every calculator, converter, and text tool on OurToolsHub is completely free to use without registration.

Do you store or track my input data?

No. Most tools process your data directly inside your browser using JavaScript. We do not store personal input values.

Are your calculations accurate?

Yes. Our tools use industry-standard formulas and reliable conversion factors. However, users should verify critical results for professional use.

Can I use these tools on my phone?

Absolutely. OurToolsHub is built with a mobile-first approach and works smoothly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Do I need to create an account?

No registration is required. You can instantly use all tools without signing up.

How do you make money if tools are free?

Our website may display advertisements or use affiliate partnerships to support operational costs.

What should I do if a tool is not working?

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue continues, please contact our support team.

Can I suggest a new tool?

Yes! We welcome feedback and tool suggestions. Please contact us via the Support page.
