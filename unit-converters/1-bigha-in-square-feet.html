What is 1 Bigha in Square Feet?

In Nepal, 1 Bigha is equal to 72,900 square feet. It is a traditional land measurement unit used mainly in the Terai region. Bigha is widely used for agriculture, real estate, and land registration.

How to Convert Bigha to Square Feet?

To convert Bigha to square feet, multiply the number of Bigha by 72,900.

Example: 2 Bigha = 2 × 72,900 = 145,800 sq ft

Where is Bigha Used in Nepal?

Bigha is mainly used in the Terai region of Nepal including districts like Morang, Sunsari, Jhapa, and Rupandehi. It is the standard unit for measuring agricultural land and large property areas.
